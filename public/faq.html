Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about artificial turf installation, maintenance, and benefits.

How long does artificial turf last?
With proper maintenance, our premium artificial turf lasts 15-20 years. It's backed by a comprehensive warranty and maintains its appearance throughout its lifespan.
Is artificial turf safe for pets and children?
Yes, our artificial turf is completely safe for pets and children. It's non-toxic, lead-free, and available with antimicrobial infill options. The surface provides excellent drainage and is easy to clean.
What's involved in the installation process?
Installation typically involves:
  • Site preparation and removal of existing grass/soil
  • Installation of proper drainage system
  • Adding crushed stone base
  • Laying and securing the turf
  • Adding infill material
Most installations are completed within 2-5 days, depending on the area size.
How do I maintain artificial turf?
Maintenance is minimal and includes:
  • Regular brushing to maintain grass direction
  • Rinsing to remove dust or debris
  • Removing leaves and organic matter
  • Annual power brushing (optional service we provide)
No mowing, watering, or fertilizing required!
What are the cost savings for commercial properties?
Commercial properties typically save:
  • 75% reduction in annual maintenance costs
  • 100% elimination of irrigation needs
  • No more seasonal contracts for mowing/maintenance
  • Reduced liability from traditional lawn equipment
ROI is typically achieved within 2-3 years.
Can artificial turf be installed in winter?
Yes, we can install artificial turf year-round in the GTA, weather permitting. However, we recommend installation during spring through fall for optimal conditions. Winter installations may take longer due to ground conditions.
How does artificial turf handle Canadian winters?
Our artificial turf is specifically designed for Canadian climate:
  • Withstands freeze-thaw cycles
  • Excellent drainage for snow melt
  • UV-protected to prevent fading
  • No damage from snow removal
What warranty do you offer?
We provide comprehensive warranty coverage:
  • 15-year manufacturer warranty on turf quality
  • 5-year warranty on installation
  • 100% satisfaction guarantee
  • Professional support throughout warranty period
