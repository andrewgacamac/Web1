About Us

YARD GUARD LANDSCAPING is a team of innovating landscape design professionals, striving towards being a leader in the landscaping industry. Servicing GTA and surrounding areas for 15+ years, this young but mighty company has been designing and building functional living outdoor spaces for all types of yards. Whether big or small we can design something to fit all your needs! Not only do we create but we are professionals in maintaining yards with the most detail. Check out our gallery and 3D design projects to see what we can do. We would love the opportunity to quote you on your next project.

Meet Michael Kasowski

My passion for landscaping started at a young age. My father operated a small successful landscaping company and growing up I was very involved in the family business. I pursued this passion by achieving a diploma in Horticulture Science and Landscape Design from Humber College. It was there that I was able to mix my landscaping knowledge with 3D design. I take pride in building a company where great customer service and creating beautiful landscapes are at the forefront.

Our Approach
